# ShuttlePoseReview Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

ShuttlePoseReview is a badminton motion review app. It lets users select a local badminton video and generate an on-device motion review with pose overlay, power-action timeline, and training suggestions.

## Data Collection

ShuttlePoseReview does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or cross-app tracking identifiers.

## Video Processing

Videos selected by the user are processed on the user's device for motion review. The selected video and generated review results remain local to the device unless the user chooses to share or export them through system sharing features.

## Photos Access

The app requests access only so the user can choose a local badminton video for analysis. Access is used for the selected video import flow.
